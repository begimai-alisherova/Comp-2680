Our Story

Welcome to Common Grounds Coffee Shop, where sustainability and community come together in every cup. Our journey began with a simple idea: to create a space on campus where students could enjoy exceptional coffee and food while making a positive impact on the environment and their fellow students.

Founded and run by students, Common Grounds is more than just a coffee shop. It's a commitment to ethical and sustainable practices. Our story is one of fair trade, organic beans sourced from cooperatives and roasted locally for a fresh, rich flavor. It's about using 100% renewable resources for our coffee and soup cups, which compost in as little as 45 days.

Join us in the journey towards a better world, one cup at a time. Discover our menu of Bean Around the World coffees and fresh, locally sourced food options. Explore the ways you can make a difference with us through our membership benefits and our commitment to sustainability.

At Common Grounds, our story is about brewing change, and we invite you to be a part of it.
